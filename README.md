## 🌊 &nbsp;About Me

<img align="right" width="220" style="margin-left:20px;" src="https://64.media.tumblr.com/90f6cdcbc51c53fa4ca7294da96fc3f8/tumblr_pios19KpQC1ufq8wko1_1280.gif" alt="moon night sky" />
I'm **Angela Jahziel "AJ" Encabo** — a BSCS-2 student at Cebu Institute of Technology–University. I build software at the intersection of technology, creativity, and human impact. My works are shaped by one question:

> *"Does this technology make someone's life better?"*

That question has driven me to build AI-assisted diagnostics for early disease detection, child safety tools that earned recognition in Cebu City, and narrative games that explore Filipino culture and the complexity of human relationships.

Thus, I believe the best software is:
- 🌱 **Human-centered** — built around people, not just features
- 🌎 **Impact-driven** — solving problems that actually matter
- ✨ **Inclusive** — accessible to the communities it serves
- 🧩 **Versatile** — not bound by a single discipline or stack
